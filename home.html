PURPOSE

Since the school promotes overall development of student we encourage students to join the clubs they want including extra cirricular ones. Since many students are facing problems in managing their time and studies with those clubs . We can understand at this level it is quite difficult to priortise some activities .So to provide them academic aid school has taken initiative of support study groups

CHILDREN WHO CAN JOIN :

All children from class 6-12 can join this support study group.

PATHSHALA

These are small teams that students can form for their own help and group study maximum team strength is 4 and each team will be alloted a teacher for help
